💡 HOW IRON CONDORS WORK (Quick Recap)

Instead of betting on BIG directional moves, iron condors profit from "NOT moving much":

Traditional Naked Call:
  • Need: NVDA to move to $160+ for profit
  • Time decay works AGAINST you
  • Win rate: ~55%

Iron Condor @ NVDA $150:
  • Sell $155 Call / Buy $160 Call (top half)
  • Sell $145 Put / Buy $140 Put (bottom half)
  • Profit if stock stays $145-$155
  • Collect premium upfront, exit at 50% profit in 3-5 days
  • Time decay works FOR you daily
  • Win rate: ~75-80%

Result: Same capital, higher probability, smoother returns.
